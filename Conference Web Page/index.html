What is This Website?

  • Registration Page: This page allows participants to sign up for the conference. They can fill out a form (like a Google Form) to provide their details and register for the event.
  • Program Page: On this page, you'll find the schedule for the conference. It lists the times and topics of presentations, workshops, and other activities happening during the event.
  • Directions Page: This page provides clear instructions on how to get to the conference venue. It includes information on transportation options, parking, and any other relevant details to help attendees find their way.
  • Promotional Materials Page: Here, you can find flyers and other promotional materials for the conference. These materials can be downloaded by participants who want to help spread the word about the event.

You can use this template by copying all the files from a place on the internet called GitHub. This template is based on a website I made for a conference called Nilesh. If you want to see how this template looks in action, you can check out the MathConnections 2019 website.
