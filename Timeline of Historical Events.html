Timeline of Significant Historical Events

18th Century

  1. 1776: American Declaration of Independence
  2. 1789: French Revolution begins

19th Century

  1. 1804: Napoleon crowns himself Emperor of the French
  2. 1861-1865: American Civil War
  3. 1889: Eiffel Tower completed

20th Century

  1. 1903: Wright brothers' first powered flight
  2. 1912: Titanic sinks
  3. 1914-1918: World War I
  4. 1929: Wall Street Crash and the Great Depression
  5. 1945: End of World War II with the dropping of atomic bombs on Hiroshima and Nagasaki
  6. 1947: India gains independence from British rule
  7. 1969: Apollo 11 moon landing
  8. 1989: Fall of the Berlin Wall

21st Century

  1. 2001: September 11 attacks
  2. 2007: Release of the first iPhone
  3. 2011: Arab Spring movements
  4. 2020: COVID-19 pandemic
